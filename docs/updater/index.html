Tidesy Firmware Updater

Update your Tidesy device directly from your browser via USB

Your browser does not support Web Serial. Please use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge on a desktop computer.

How it works

  1. Plug your Tidesy device into this computer via USB
  2. Select your board model below
  3. Click Install and choose the serial port
  4. Wait for the update to complete — your device will restart automatically
